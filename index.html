Online Spanish Tuition

About Me

I’m a Spanish tutor with over 10 years of teaching experience. I provide online Spanish lessons to anyone who wishes to learn or to improve this language.

Please get in contact to ask any questions and discuss what you hope to get out of the lessons. Thank you.

Profile

¡Hola amigos! I am originally from Madrid and I have lived in the UK for over 7 years. I am a qualified teacher and have completed a 4 year teaching degree in Madrid. While studying at uni I used to tutor different subjects to students in both primary and secondary education. After finishing my degree, I was awarded with a grant to travel to the UK to teach Spanish within three secondary schools. I ended up in Londonderry, Northern Ireland which was such an amazing experience that made me realise I wanted to work as a Spanish teacher.

When the contract ended, I moved to Oxford to work in a nursery while I kept studying English in order to reach the accreditation I was aiming for. After I achieved it, I went back to Spain for few months to plan my next step: moving to North Wales. And there is where my dream came true. Finally, I had the chance to start providing Spanish lessons to all kind of people and ages, from totally beginners to advanced students.

Lessons

I tailor each lesson to the needs of my students and plan each lesson to be as interesting and effective as possible. Before starting any lessons I arrange a videochat beforehand to discuss your level and desires from the lessons, allowing me to plan accordingly.
